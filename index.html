Field notes · El Paso County, TX · FIPS 48141

Where the jobs land,
where the workers live.

Between 2011 and 2023, El Paso County's job map shifted. This survey lays two federal labor datasets side by side — LEHD LODES commuting flows and QWI job creation records — to trace where new employment took root, and where the people who fill those jobs come home to at night.

Sources: U.S. Census Bureau LEHD Origin-Destination Employment Statistics (LODES v8) and Quarterly Workforce Indicators (QWI). LODES counts show worker home/workplace pairs at a point in time and do not by themselves distinguish new job creation from relocation of existing jobs; QWI job creation/destruction figures are the more reliable measure of new employment. Home tract change figures reflect employer location only and do not account for population growth in a tract generally.
